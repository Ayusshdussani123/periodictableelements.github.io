Magnesium

Element Overview

Atomic Number: 12

Atomic Mass: 24.305 u

Symbol: Mg

Classification: Metal

Family Name: Alkaline Earth Metals

Period: 3

Physical Properties

State at Room Temperature: Solid

Color and Appearance: Silvery-white

Density: 1.738 g/cm³

Melting Point: 650 °C

Boiling Point: 1090 °C

Chemical Properties

Common Compounds: MgO, MgCl₂, Mg(OH)₂

Reactivity: Reacts with acids to produce hydrogen gas, burns with a bright white flame

Uses: Found in alloys, fireworks, and as a dietary supplement

Interesting Facts

Discovery: Discovered in 1755 by Joseph Black

Unique Properties: Essential for human health, used in lightweight alloys
